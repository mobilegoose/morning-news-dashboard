## 2. Most Useful Development Today

### A new cyclospora outbreak is under investigation

Federal officials are investigating 72 illnesses in a cluster whose food source has not been identified; it is separate from the earlier Taylor Farms outbreak. Severe or persistent watery diarrhea warrants medical advice and mention of possible food exposure. Handwashing and cooking food to at least 158°F can reduce risk, although washing produce may not remove the parasite completely. [AP, July 24](https://apnews.com/article/cyclospora-outbreak-fda-diarrhea-8eb2e66498136ea4dd170118ef1a8a4e)

### New Free Journal Articles

#### [Item comprehension and content coverage of patient-reported outcome measures for long COVID: a qualitative study](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s11136-026-04340-7)

**Quality of Life Research · Online July 23, 2026 · Peer-reviewed qualitative study.** Virtual cognitive-debriefing interviews with 30 English-speaking adults with Long COVID found most measures understandable and relevant, but wording, instructions, length and cognitive burden caused problems. This matters for trials and disability/function documentation. **Limitation:** small, English-speaking sample; measurement study, not treatment research. [Free full text](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s11136-026-04340-7)

#### [Impact of an 18-month non-native language intervention on episodic memory and the underlying neural mechanisms in cognitively unimpaired older adults](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC13392915/)

**Alzheimer’s & Dementia (Amsterdam) · Online July 23, 2026 · Secondary randomized-trial analysis.** Among 89 cognitively unimpaired adults age 65 or older, delayed recall was maintained in the language-training group and declined in controls; change correlated with hippocampal perfusion. **Limitation:** modest secondary analysis that does not establish Alzheimer prevention or treatment. [Free full text at PubMed Central](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC13392915/)

#### [Troponin T and Neurofilament Light Chain Levels as Complementary Biomarkers of Disease Accumulation and Aggressiveness in Amyotrophic Lateral Sclerosis](https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/full/10.1002/acn3.70499)

**Annals of Clinical and Translational Neurology · Online July 23, 2026 · Retrospective observational study.** German discovery and replication cohorts included 433 and 185 people. Troponin T tracked accumulated stage while serum neurofilament light better reflected aggressiveness. **Limitation:** retrospective findings require prospective longitudinal validation before routine prognostic use. [Free full text](https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/full/10.1002/acn3.70499)

**Also new:** A review of six randomized focal-epilepsy trials involving 961 participants found no strong evidence that adding versus switching medication after a first drug fails produces better remission; the old, heterogeneous, mostly high-bias evidence shows the need for modern trials. [Epileptic Disorders, online July 24 — free full text](https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/full/10.1002/epd2.70353)
