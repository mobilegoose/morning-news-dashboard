DOJ activates the Alien Terrorist Removal Court for the first time

The Justice Department filed the first petition in the special court created in 1996 for terrorism-related removal cases. The matter is sealed; the court ordered the government to supply additional legal and factual support.

FDA says a previously reported positive lettuce sample was a false positive. The investigation remains open, and consumers are still told not to eat recalled Taylor Farms de Mexico iceberg lettuce. Tennessee is among the listed distribution states.

FDA investigation · CDC outbreak notice
